About

The Tavern Bot is a discord bot used to play besides D&D 5th edition. Its core functionality exists out of rolling dice, looking up content and showing memes. The Tavern Bot has a wide variety of commands that will make your 5th edition experience much smoother. It can deal with more complicated dice type, look up spells and recalculate your currency.
